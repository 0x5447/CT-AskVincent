About Nutmeg Navigator

Welcome to Nutmeg Navigator, your free AI companion for uncovering the best of Connecticut—without a trace. Powered by Venice AI, this chatbot is here to help visitors and locals alike explore fun things to do, from Mystic’s seaside charm to Hartford’s hidden gems, all with one big promise: your chats stay yours.

We don’t capture a single byte of your data. Nothing’s stored, nothing’s tracked, and definitely nothing’s sold. Ask away—your questions vanish into the ether once you’re done, leaving you free to roam the Nutmeg State without a digital shadow.

Born in 2025 from a love for Connecticut’s quirks and a passion for privacy, Nutmeg Navigator is a self-funded labor of love. It’s all about sparking joy in your journey, whether you’re here for a weekend or a lifetime. So, dive in, explore, and if you love the ride, consider tossing a coin our way to keep this adventure rolling!
