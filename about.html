About Vincent’s Connecticut Companion

Meet Vincent, your private AI companion powered by Venice AI, designed to elevate your Connecticut journey. Whether you’re a visitor seeking hidden gems or a local curious about your state, Vincent’s here to inspire and inform—all with 100% private conversations.

Launched in 2025, this project blends cutting-edge AI with a love for the Nutmeg State, offering a fun, accessible way to explore Connecticut’s wonders.

Dive in, ask anything, and if Vincent sparks joy in your travels, consider supporting this passion project!
